About Me

My career, life, hobbies

Born and raised in Toronto, Canada, as a single son to immigrant refugees from Vietnam. I graduated from the University of Toronto, majoring in Chemistry, and currently working on my Masters degree in Mechanical Engineering at York University.

Although my career is focused on STEM, I have a strong desire to cultivate my creative, more artistic side. For this reason I have decided to learn how to create my own website, as a platform for myself. I do this for me, and by doing so hope to inspire anyone with the myriad of thoughts, hobbies, and experiences I share. 

B_Truo is simply my name, shortened to look a bit cooler, but also sends a message: be true. I will expand upon my thoughts on what being true encapsulates, but for now, take a deep breath, and enjoy your stay.
